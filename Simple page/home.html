Religious Tourism


Religious tourism is one of the oldest types of tourism and its goal is to perform religious rituals, as is the case with Muslims, such as Hajj and Umrah, as well as recreation and expansion for family members as stated in the teachings of Islam.


Cultural tourism


Cultural tourism is a subset of tourism concerned with the traveler's engagement with the culture of a country or region, specifically the lifestyle of the people in those geographic areas, the history of those people, their art, architecture, religion(s), and other elements that have contributed to shaping their way of life.


Medical tourism


Medical tourism is one of the types of tourism, which is travel for the purpose of treatment or recreation in health resorts in various parts of the world.


Coastal tourism


It is the most popular form of tourism activities recognized in the countries of the world, and is mainly associated with coastal resorts Beach tourism with mountain tourism constitutes the most important types of popular tourism or mass tourism, and it often includes a health and sports dimension such as skiing, sand tennis, meditation and kayaking.
